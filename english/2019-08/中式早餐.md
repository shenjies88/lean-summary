### 例句

- Well since they're not very common back home, we don't have a name for them
  - 我们那儿很少有这些东西，所以也没有对应的名字来称呼它
- it's my favorite on-the-go breakfast
  - 这是我最喜欢的便携早餐
- I guess you could, but it's really more like a wrap
  - 大概可以吧，但是煎饼更像卷饼

- More like
